The Process

How we run your email campaign.

A proven five-step system built on thousands of campaigns. Every step is documented, measured, and handed back to you transparently.

1

Discovery & ICP Alignment

We kick off with a deep-dive session to understand your product, positioning, and ideal buyer. We document the ICP with enough precision to build targeted lists and write copy that resonates — not just demographic filters.

2

Infrastructure Setup

We provision sending domains (separate from your main domain), configure SPF, DKIM, and DMARC, set up email accounts, and run a 2-week warm-up sequence before the first send. This protects your deliverability from day one.

3

List Building & Copy

We build and verify your prospect list, then write the full sequence — subject lines, openers, value props, CTAs, and follow-ups. Each email is reviewed against deliverability and spam-trigger checks before going live.

4

Campaign Launch

Campaigns go live with controlled sending volumes to protect sender reputation. We monitor deliverability, open rates, and bounce rates daily — pausing and adjusting if anything looks off.

5

Optimize & Scale

Week over week we A/B test subjects, angles, and CTAs. When we find a winner, we scale volume. You get a weekly report with results, changes, and next steps.
