[02] What's included

Everything to run cold email at scale.

Domain & Infrastructure

Dedicated sending domains that mirror your primary domain. SPF, DKIM, DMARC configured. Inbox warm-up over 3–4 weeks before volume starts.

ICP & Prospect Lists

We define your ideal customer profile and build verified lists of decision-makers who match. No scraped junk — each contact checked for validity before sending.

Sequence & Copywriting

Multi-touch sequences written to earn attention, not demand it. Touch one earns a read. Touch three earns a reply. Personalization at every step.

Deliverability Monitoring

We track inbox placement, spam rates, and domain reputation daily. If something changes, we catch it before it hurts your program.

A/B Testing & Optimization

Subject lines, openers, and CTAs tested continuously. We run experiments on every campaign and apply learnings to the next batch automatically.

Weekly Reporting

Reply rates, meeting rate, pipeline created — four numbers every week in plain language. You always know what's working and what's changing.

Built for B2B companies ready to scale.

Email outbound works best for companies with a clear ICP, a product or service with a defined value proposition, and a sales process that can handle qualified inbound conversations. If you're trying to reach decision-makers at scale without building an internal SDR function, this is the motion.
