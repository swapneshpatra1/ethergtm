What It Is

Where your buyers already spend their time.

LinkedIn is where B2B decision-makers share wins, announce promotions, and talk about their problems. That context is pure gold for outbound — if you know how to use it.

Most LinkedIn outbound fails because it feels like outbound. We make it feel like a natural conversation — researching each prospect, crafting messages that show genuine interest, and following up in a way that builds rapport rather than burning it.

The Process

How we run your LinkedIn campaign.

From profile audit to booked meetings — a systematic approach to LinkedIn outbound that protects your reputation while filling your calendar.

1

Profile Audit & Optimization

Before we send a single message, we audit your LinkedIn profile and rewrite it to position you as a credible, value-driven expert in your space. Prospects check your profile before replying — it needs to convert.

2

ICP & Targeting Strategy

We define your ideal LinkedIn prospect using a combination of firmographic filters and behavioral signals — recent job changes, post activity, company growth — then build high-quality prospect lists via Sales Navigator.

3

Copywriting & Sequence Build

We write connection request notes and message sequences tailored to each target segment. Each message is short, relevant, and adds a reason to respond — not another "I'd love to connect" template.

4

Campaign Execution & Inbox Management

Campaigns run within LinkedIn's safe activity limits. We monitor connection rates and reply rates daily, manage replies in your inbox, and escalate hot leads to your team with full conversation context.

5

Optimize & Scale

We test different message angles, targeting segments, and follow-up timing each week. Winners get scaled, losers get cut. You get a full report every Monday with results and the plan for the week ahead.
