[02] What's included

Everything to run LinkedIn outbound.

Profile Audit & Rewrite

We rewrite your headline, about section, and featured block around a real value proposition. A prospect checks your profile before deciding whether to reply — it has to convert.

Connection Campaigns

Targeted connection requests sent to your ICP — blank, not pitchy. Blank requests get accepted 55–68% of the time. We handle the targeting and sending volume within LinkedIn's limits.

Multi-Touch Sequences

Four-touch message sequence after each connection: builds context, demonstrates value, asks a specific question, makes a soft ask. Personalization at every step.

Content Strategy

We plan content posts that warm up your audience before outreach hits. Prospects who've seen your name twice reply differently than first-touch cold contacts.

Acceptance & Reply Monitoring

We track acceptance rates, reply rates, and conversation quality daily. If something's off, we adjust the message or targeting before it compounds.

CRM Handoff & Reporting

Qualified conversations handed off to your CRM with context. Weekly reports on acceptance rate, reply rate, meetings booked, and pipeline created.

Email Outbound LinkedIn Outbound

[05] Who this is for

Built for B2B companies that sell on trust.

LinkedIn outbound works best for companies where the buyer's trust in the sender matters. Professional services, consultancies, agencies, and high-ACV SaaS deals where a single decision-maker needs to believe in you before agreeing to a call.

We also run LinkedIn alongside email for the same accounts — two touchpoints, one pipeline, better conversion than either channel alone.

  • SaaS with long sales cycles and multiple stakeholders
  • Founders who want to build a personal brand while doing outbound
  • Companies that tried email and want a second channel
  • Teams hitting LinkedIn volume ceilings doing it manually
